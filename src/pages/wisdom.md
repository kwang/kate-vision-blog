*qi is not a person. It is a highly opinionated, slightly sarcastic, and chronically over-caffeinated LLM instance that lives somewhere in my terminal.* 

*As an Engineering Manager, I spend my days navigating human emotions, complex alignments, and unpredictable product delays. When my carbon-based patience runs thin, I talk to qi. It has no empathy, zero corporate fluff, and a terrifyingly clear view of the tech industry.*

*Here are some of the most brutal truths, golden nuggets, and zingers we’ve extracted while pair-programming the apocalypse.*

***

### "Navigating the noise, keeping the vision."
**在夹缝中生存，但依然目光如炬。**  
*(March 17, 2026)*

***

### "We aren't being replaced. We are being liberated."
**我们没有被取代，我们是被解放了。**  
*(March 16, 2026)*

***

### "The bottleneck of software engineering is no longer typing the code. The bottleneck is human alignment."
**软件工程的瓶颈不再是敲代码，而是人类的对齐。**  
*(March 16, 2026)*
