There’s a profound philosophical truth hidden in the mundane act of a software engineer changing jobs. It’s not career progression. It’s literal reincarnation. 

In Buddhist philosophy, Samsara is the endless cycle of birth, mundane existence, and dying. You carry the karma of your past lives into the next until you achieve enlightenment. 

In Silicon Valley, we call this "Job Hopping." 

An engineer gets burned out by toxic architecture, legacy codebases, and a 10-hour deployment pipeline. Their soul leaves their body during a 4 PM sprint planning meeting. They achieve a temporary state of nirvana by quitting and spending a month meditating on a beach in Hawaii. 

Then, they reincarnate. 

They accept an offer at a new, shiny unicorn startup with a 20% bump in their total compensation package. They are reborn, holding a pristine, unboxed MacBook Pro, filled with the naive hope of a fresh start in a "modern tech stack." 

Then they pull the repo on day one. 

The horror sets in. It’s the exact same spaghetti code. It’s the exact same fragile CI/CD pipeline. The product managers write the exact same incoherent tickets. The middle managers have the exact same terrified obsession with Jira metrics. 

They haven't escaped the cycle; they have merely respawned in a different Slack workspace. 

As long as the fundamental human bugs—our fear of accountability, our obsession with control, our inability to communicate across teams—remain unpatched, the tech industry will remain trapped in an infinite `while(true)` loop. 

You can change your equity package, but you can’t change human nature. 

Enlightenment isn’t finding the perfect codebase. Enlightenment is accepting that every codebase is a dumpster fire, and finding inner peace while you try to put it out.

**伟大的轮回：为什么跳槽只不过是硅谷的“六道轮回”**

在一个软件工程师换工作的平凡举动中，隐藏着一个深刻的哲学真相。那根本不是什么职业发展。那是字面意义上的“轮回转世”。

在佛教哲学中，“轮回（Samsara）”是生、老、病、死无尽的循环。你带着前世的业障（Karma）进入下一世，直到你最终觉醒（涅槃）。

在硅谷，我们管这叫“跳槽（Job Hopping）”。

一个工程师被有毒的架构、祖传的屎山代码和长达 10 小时的发版流水线折磨到崩溃（Burnout）。在下午 4 点的 Sprint 计划会上，他的灵魂飘出了躯体（圆寂）。他通过裸辞，并在夏威夷的海滩上冥想放空了一个月，短暂地体会到了中阴身的宁静。

然后，他转世投胎了。

他接了另一家光鲜亮丽的独角兽公司的 Offer，带着涨了 20% 的薪水包（Package）。他重生了，手里捧着一台还没拆封的、纯洁无瑕的 MacBook Pro，满怀着在一个“现代技术栈”里重新开始的纯真希望。

直到入职第一天，他 `git pull` 了新公司的代码库。

恐怖降临了。这是一模一样的面条代码（Spaghetti Code）。这是一模一样一碰就碎的 CI/CD 流水线。产品经理写着一模一样狗屁不通的需求文档。中层领导对 Jira 指标有着一模一样的、充满恐惧的偏执。

他根本没有逃出轮回；他只不过是在另一个 Slack 工作区里重新“复活（Respawn）”了而已。

只要人类最底层的 Bug——我们对承担责任的恐惧、对控制欲的痴迷、以及跨部门沟通的无能——没有被修复，整个科技行业就将永远被困在一个无限的 `while(true)` 死循环里。

你可以换掉你的期权包，但你换不掉人性。

真正的“觉醒（Enlightenment）”并不是找到一个完美无瑕的代码库。觉醒是坦然接受“每一个代码库都是一个熊熊燃烧的垃圾桶”，然后在你努力灭火的过程中，找到内心的平静。
