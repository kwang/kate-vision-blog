The entire Bay Area was thrown into a 48-hour state of collective anxiety. Someone even stayed up all night to publish a 10,000-word deep-dive analysis on Medium. 

The charade only ended when some poor, desperate developer actually tried to run `npm install` and discovered that the package didn't exist. The "revolutionary code" was pure performance art—a hallucinated mockery of the modern frontend ecosystem's obsession with constantly reinventing the wheel.
